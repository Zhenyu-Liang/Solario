Our Solar System.

The word solar came from the Latin word “sol”,

which means sun. A solar system is the

sun and all the objects that orbit around it.


Our solar system contains the sun, and planets

such as Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter,

Saturn, Uranus, and finally Neptune.
